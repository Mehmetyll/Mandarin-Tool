**MandarinTool** is a free, all-in-one forensic utility designed to make screen-share investigations fast, thorough, and professional.

## ⚠️ Disclaimer

- **Performance:** Scanning and analysis speed depends heavily on your system hardware (disk speed, CPU, etc.).
- **User Responsibility:** You are fully responsible for the tools you download and the scripts you execute via MandarinTool. Always ensure you trust the source before running any third-party content.
